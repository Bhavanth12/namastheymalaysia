About the Author

Maddipatla Bhavanth

Computer Science Exchange Student @ INTI University

I am a passionate Computer Science student currently engaged in a semester exchange program at INTI International University in Nilai, Malaysia.

Throughout this period, I will be exploring the vibrant and diverse culture of Malaysia. From tasting authentic local cuisines in bustling night markets to witnessing the majesty of ancient temples and participating in colorful ethnic festivals, my goal is to document and share these rich experiences.

This project reflects my journey of merging modern web development with deep cultural appreciation, inspired by the elegant aesthetics of travel and discovery.
